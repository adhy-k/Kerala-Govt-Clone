Kerala is Becoming a Model, Four-year Degree Enters Second Phase
Kerala has entered a new phase in higher education with the successful launch of the second year of the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme. Timely exam results, updated courses, and improved teaching methods have made this academic reform a reality. This historic step is a key milestone in Kerala’s journey towards achieving global education standards and building a strong knowledge-based society and economy. Faster Results, Better Learning The Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP), launched in the 2023–2024 academic year, is progressing by offering better support for students in their studies. A major achievement of this reform is that universities have successfully published the second semester results on time, just like they did for the first semester. This academic year has started with the timely declaration of results and the introduction of a new system to ensure results are announced before the next semester begins.
